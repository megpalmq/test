AIGA Herron, the official student group of AIGA, embodies creativity, professionalism, & innovation, aiming to inspire and support a community of aspiring designers and creative thinkers.

Dedicated to advancing design excellence, AIGA Herron strives to cultivate a collaborative learning space where students can develop their skills, build meaningful connections, and emerge as leaders in the design field.
